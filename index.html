How to consume water using a 120V Constuction drill

Article Title

To consume water normally you'd put it in a cup and drink it, however some people are unable to do this and they have Constuction drills instead of cups. To consume water using a Constuction Drill, first you'd need to replace the head with a more sphere-ish metal head, then if the bottom of the drill is empty you may close it with Metal or any hard object, then simply pour in the water and drink it, for more flavor you may activate the drill to mix the water then you can drink it afterwards.
